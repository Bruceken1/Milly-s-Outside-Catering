About Us

Crafting unforgettable culinary experiences since 2018

Milly's Outside Catering was founded by Milly Akinyi in Mombasa with a simple dream: to bring restaurant-quality food and warm, professional service to outdoor events across Kenya.

From intimate beach weddings in Diani to large corporate galas in Nairobi, we have proudly catered over 850 events. Our passion lies in using the freshest local ingredients, creating custom menus, and delivering flawless execution every single time.
