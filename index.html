About Us

Crafting unforgettable culinary experiences since 2011

Milly's Outside Catering was founded by Milly Okina in Busia with a simple dream: to bring restaurant-quality food and warm, professional service to outdoor events across Kenya.

From weddings to large corporate events, we have proudly catered over 850 events. Our passion lies in using the freshest local ingredients, creating custom menus, and delivering flawless execution every single time.
